1. Introduction

Welcome to The Hormone Method ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use The Hormone Method mobile application.

Please read this policy carefully. By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1A. Accounts and Login Security

The Hormone Method requires an account to use the App, created with your email address and a password.

How your password is protected: Your password is never stored in plain text. It is hashed using industry-standard encryption before it touches our database, meaning even our own team cannot view it. If you forget your password, we can only help you reset it — we cannot look it up or tell it to you.

Session handling: When you log in, we issue a secure session token (not your password) to keep you logged in on your device. This token is stored securely and expires automatically after a period of inactivity.

What you can control:

  • Change your password at any time in Settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time (see Section 7.2)

We will never email you asking for your password. If you receive such a request claiming to be from us, please report it to hellohormonemethod@gmail.com and do not respond.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use the App, you may provide:

  • Account information: First name, email address, and password
  • Health and wellness information: Hormonal stage, cycle phase, symptoms, health goals, and morning time availability
  • Daily tracking data: Energy levels, mood scores, sleep quality ratings, anxiety levels, period days, and symptoms you log each day
  • Habit completion data: Whether you completed your morning ritual, walk, supplements, breathwork, or other daily practices
  • AI coach conversations: Messages you send to and receive from the AI coaching feature, including any you choose to save

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information: Device type and operating system version
  • Usage data: Features you access and general interaction patterns, used only to improve the App

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Social Security numbers or government identification
  • Financial account numbers or credit card information
  • Precise GPS or real-time location data
  • Contacts, photos, or camera data
  • Lab results, prescription information, or connections to electronic health records
  • Insurance information

2.4 Apple Health (Optional)

If you choose to connect the App to Apple Health, sleep data you've granted access to may be read from Apple Health to pre-fill your sleep log. This connection is entirely optional, requires your explicit permission through iOS, and can be revoked at any time in your device's Settings app. We do not write any data back to Apple Health.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalize the App: Deliver phase-specific guidance, supplement information, movement plans, breathwork, and mindfulness practices tailored to your profile and track
  • Power the AI coach: Your health profile and conversation history are used to generate personalized coaching responses via the Anthropic API
  • Save your progress: Store your daily tracking logs, streak, and history across sessions
  • Send local reminders: If you enable them, daily reminder notifications are scheduled directly on your device — see Section 5.1 for detail on how this works
  • Improve the App: Analyze aggregate, anonymized usage patterns to fix issues and improve features
  • Communicate with you: Send account-related emails such as password resets or policy updates

We do not use your health data for advertising purposes. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Health Data and HIPAA Notice

The Hormone Method collects health-related information including menstrual cycle data, hormonal symptoms, mood, sleep quality, energy levels, and wellness practices. This information is sensitive, and we treat it with the highest level of care.

Important: The Hormone Method is a consumer wellness application and is not a covered entity or business associate as defined under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). The health information you enter into this App is not protected by HIPAA.

However, we voluntarily apply privacy principles consistent with HIPAA's spirit, including collecting only necessary health data, not disclosing it without your consent, implementing technical safeguards, and giving you control over your data.

We strongly encourage you not to enter highly sensitive medical information (such as prescription medication details or diagnoses from healthcare providers) into the App.

5. How We Share Your Information

5.1 Service Providers

We work with a small number of trusted providers to operate the App. These providers only access your data to perform services on our behalf and are contractually prohibited from using it for other purposes:

  • Anthropic (AI coach): Your AI coach messages and health profile context are processed by Anthropic's API to generate coaching responses. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Supabase (database): Your account data and tracking logs are stored securely in Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Resend (email delivery): Used only to deliver account-related emails, such as signup confirmation and password resets.

A note on reminder notifications: Daily reminders are scheduled locally on your own device using Apple's built-in notification system. No third-party notification service is used, and no data about your reminders or app usage is sent to an outside notification provider.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of The Hormone Method, our users, or the public.

5.3 Business Transfers

If The Hormone Method is acquired, merged, or its assets are transferred, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice in the App before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Your account information and health tracking data are stored on secure servers, not only on your device. This is what allows you to see your patterns and progress over time, avoid losing data if you switch phones, and lets your AI coach maintain conversation context.

What stays local to your device only: Biometric data used to unlock the App (such as Face ID) is handled entirely by iOS and never sent to our servers. Scheduled reminder notifications also live entirely on your device.

6.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit and at rest: All data moving between your device and our servers is encrypted (TLS 1.2+), and all data is encrypted at rest.
  • Row-level security: Access controls are enforced at the database level. The database itself blocks any attempt to access another user's data — a structural safeguard, not merely a policy.
  • Restricted access: Access to production data is limited to essential personnel.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6.3 Data Breach Notification

If we become aware of a security incident affecting your personal data, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of confirming the breach, describe what data was affected, and outline steps you can take to protect yourself.

6.4 Vulnerability Reporting

If you discover a security issue with the App, please email hellohormonemethod@gmail.com rather than disclosing it publicly.

6.5 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it longer.

To exercise any GDPR right, contact hellohormonemethod@gmail.com.
